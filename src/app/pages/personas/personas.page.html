personas

A buyer persona might look something like this:

  • Name: David
  • Demographics: Married, stay-at-home dad with kids between the ages of three and eight. Homeowner, middle-class, and located in the Pacific Northwest.
  • Goals: To find clothing for kids that is within budget and update their wardrobe.
  • Challenges: Keeping his kids away from television and video games and occupying their time while he completes other tasks around the house.
  • Purchase behavior: New customer; hasn’t made a purchase from us before.
  • Interests and hobbies: He spends a lot of time on Instagram and engaging with people on forums.
  • Benefits of my product: All clothing is designed on an affordable budget and is categorized based on age.
  • Objections: He doesn’t want to buy just clothing that his kids will get tired of and doesn’t want to waste time in exploring stores.
